## Origin & Evolution

This repository began as a training log. Over time, it has evolved into a structured research archive for **Neuro-Linguistic Data Mining (NLDM)** – the study of how linguistic structures (especially the 6 primal pronouns) mirror combinatorial geometry ($2^6 = 64$) and stabilize human interaction under environmental frequency pressure.
